About me

H ello, My name is Tim Sweetsir, newly published author in December 2019, my first book, "Killer in the Dusk" This book was written in 2018 and edited by my wife, and now Co-Author of quite a few books in the works. Our second book is actually a sequel to "Killer in the Dusk" The second book is "Forest Ridge Murders" Look for that one in early 2021 right here on Amazon. I am a 63-year-old Retired/Veteran. My wife Debra, as I said, is not only my editor, she is now a co-author on several upcoming books. We have 3 adult children Cory, James, and Christopher, James is married to Alexandra and together they have two children Emily who will be (5) in June 2021, and Marshall who just joined this world in October 2020. We hope you enjoy our book and all future books

"Thanks for reading! If you enjoyed this book, I'd be very grateful if you'd post a short review on Amazon. Your support really does make a difference and I read all the reviews personally. Your feedback is greatly appreciated and it does make our Books even better.

Thanks again for your support!"

Tim Sweetsir
